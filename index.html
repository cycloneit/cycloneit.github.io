Welcome to Cyclone IT

We provide IT solutions for Agri-food products and traceability using blockchain. Cyclone IT believes blockchain technology is the future of the Agri-food process, helping producers to better manage the quality and safety of their products for their consumers.

Strengthen your Brand

Producers can increase their marketability by using the traceability information to verify their product’s quality. Consumers opt for a product for its distinct qualities, such as its geographical indications (GI), sustainable/ethical production, its organic nature, or because it is a non-genetically modified food.

Product digital identity

Every product is assigned with a unique and traceable digital identity such as a QR code, data matrix, NFC/RFID tag etc.The consumer will uses it to access to all the information on the origin and processing methods of fruits, milk, eggs, cheese and meat...
